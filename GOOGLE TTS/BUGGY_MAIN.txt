
In the world of One Piece, becoming the Pirate King is the ultimate dream. It is the title that represents absolute freedom, legendary strength, and the ability to conquer the most dangerous sea in the world — the Grand Line.
For decades, pirates have fought, betrayed each other, and risked their lives chasing the treasure left behind by Gol D. Roger, the only man who ever held that title.
Naturally, when fans think about the next Pirate King, a few obvious names come to mind.
The first is Monkey D. Luffy, the fearless captain of the Straw Hat Pirates whose entire journey is built around reaching the final island. Others might consider powerful legends like Shanks, or terrifying masterminds like Marshall D. Teach.
But there is one name that almost nobody would seriously mention in that conversation…
Buggy.
